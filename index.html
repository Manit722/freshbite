Our Story

A small kitchen with big intentions.

FreshBite started in 2019 above a bookshop in Hazratganj — a single espresso machine, two cane chairs and a hand-written menu. Today, our kitchen sources from twelve regional farms across Uttar Pradesh, our beans are roasted weekly, and our pastry counter still opens at exactly 7:30 in the morning.

Every plate we send out is the answer to one quiet question: would we proudly serve this to our family on a Sunday?
